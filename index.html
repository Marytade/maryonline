Hello, world! My name is Mary Adebayo. I am a developer, UX designer, and multidisciplinary artist based in Houston, TX.

About Me

Like many early-2000s kids who grew up on the internet, playing Nintendo games, watching cartoons on cable TV, and scrolling on their Blackberry, I've always loved being around technology. I fondly remember creating Tumblr collages with my sister on hot summer days and listening to the radio with my six siblings, the AC cranked up in our small apartment.

I often found myself creating, building and designing, and eventually selling the things I made. Development, programming, and web-building later on became one of the many tools I use to support my creativity.
